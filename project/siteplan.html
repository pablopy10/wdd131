School Management SaaS - Website Plan

Site Name

Site Name: AngoSchool

This name was chosen because it clearly conveys the purpose of the software: managing educational institutions effectively. The name is short, professional, and easily memorable for schools and administrators from Angola.

Optional Domain: angoschool.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the AngoSchool website is to provide a comprehensive platform for schools and educational institutions to manage operations in Angola. The website will showcase the features of the SaaS platform, including:

  • Student information management
  • Teacher and staff scheduling
  • Fee management and financial tracking
  • Real-time communication tools for parents, teachers, and students
  • Reporting and analytics for school administrators

Scenarios

  • How can I track student attendance efficiently?
  • What are the pricing plans for EduManage?
  • How can parents access their child's academic progress reports?

Color Schema

Selected Colors:

  • #2a66d9: Used for headings, links, and primary buttons.
  • #f4f6f8: Background color for the site.
  • #333: Text for body paragraphs and descriptions.

Typography

  • Arial, sans-serif: Used for body text and headings to maintain simplicity and readability.
  • Roboto, sans-serif: Used for emphasis in pricing tables and feature highlights.
